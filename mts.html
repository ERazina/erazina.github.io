About project

This internal web platform was developed for MTS employees as a single entry point to over 20 different corporate systems (HR, finance, legal, document management, etc.). The primary objective was to replace fragmented access with a centralized and intuitive interface, eliminating the need to log into and navigate through multiple separate systems. By consolidating various services into one cohesive frontend, the platform significantly improved efficiency, ensured SSO-based access, and offered a personalized experience with widgets, dashboards, and service shortcuts.
